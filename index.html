WAYNE TOWER

CENTER OF GOTHAM CITY

About

Wayne Tower is a Building in Downtown Gotham City known as Old Gotham that was built in 1888 and Served as Main Office of Wayne Enterprises until it was moved to its New Headquaters in Dark Knight.


It is Owned by Wayne Enterprises and also called "Old Wayne Tower" and "Wayne Industries Headquaters". It is built as a symbol to welcome all of those who Enter the City

Origins

When Solomon Wayne saw the Cyrus Pinkney's sketches he knew he had found the style for a new kind of building, unfortunately the tallest of the buildings, the tower, was unfeasable at the time. Later, as new building materials and technologies (tieing a building's foundation into the bedrock) were developed, it became possible to make that vision a reality.


Solomon's son, Alan Wayne, working with other important members of Gotham elite pushed for the construction of a tower that would stand as inspiration to the good people of Gotham. It was constructed in 1888 looking much like Pinkney's original drawing of it. Many other "Gotham style" skyscapers folowed.

Key Highlights

Tallest Building in Gotham

Over a 1000 ft tall, with 78 floors the tower is the tallest building in Gotham City


13 Gargoyles to Welcome Visitors

It has 13 gargoyles, welcome visitors for each of the 13 entry points into the city. The 5 guardians on the 1st tier watch the original 5 gateways into Gotham, the 3 bridges and 2 tunnels. The 7 guardians watch the 7 train lines that converge below. This last one, watches the airport.


Union Station

This Station is Main Station of Gotham City Located in Tower's Base, there are 7 train lines from different parts of city.


Electrical Substation

All of Gotham's electricity is controlled by the substation located under the building.


Observation Deck

At the top of the tower is an observation deck which is open to the public for free every weekend. It has double bonded laminated float glass for windows that are crystal-quality, weatherproof, and unbreakable.


Water Mains

All of Gotham's Water is controlled by the Water Mains located under the building.
